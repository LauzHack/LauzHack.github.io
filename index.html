F.A.Q.

Can I participate?

You must be at least 18 years old and a student, or have graduated from university in the past twelve months.

How much does it cost?

Thanks to our sponsors, attendance is completely free.
This also includes meals.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger and other gadgets you might use for your project.
Please bring a valid ID for admission.

Where will I sleep?

We have a sleeping area with mattresses, and showers.
Please bring your sleeping bag, towel, and toiletries.

What will I eat?

We'll provide meals from Saturday breakfast to Sunday lunch.

I've never been to a hackathon...

We are very beginner friendly.
There will be plenty of mentors and a range of workshops to help you out.

I don't have a team...

Don't worry! Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting new people.
You'll have time before hacking to socialize and form teams of up to 4.
