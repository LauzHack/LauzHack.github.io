LauzHack

November 19-20, 2016
EPFL, Switzerland

Pre-register now!

An invention marathon.
Learn, build & share during a weekend in a relaxed atmosphere.
No need to be a programmer, anybody can come!

EPFL, the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne, is one of Europe's top universities.
It is located in the southwestern part of Switzerland, bordering Lake Geneva.

F.A.Q.

Is it free?

Yes! Anybody can come for free.
We will also provide food and drinks.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop and a power cord!
You may also want to bring headphones.

Can I sleep there?

There will be beds and showers available.
Please bring your own toiletries and towels.

Do I own my hack?

Yes.
Anything you create will be owned solely by you.

How do teams work?

You will work in teams of up to 5 participants.
If you do not have a team, we will start with team building.
