F.A.Q.

Can I participate?

You must be at least 18 years old and a student, or have graduated from university in the past twelve months.

How much does it cost?

Thanks to our sponsors, attendance is completely free.
This also includes meals.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger and other gadgets you might use for your project.
Please bring a valid ID for admission.

Where will I sleep?

We'll have a few bean bags in a quiet room for you to nap at the venue.
Sleep well beforehand, and feel free to bring a sleeping bag.
You'll have access to showers; please bring your own towel and toiletries.

What will I eat?

We'll provide meals from breakfast on Saturday to lunch on Sunday.
